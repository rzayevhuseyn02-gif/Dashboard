EWS (Early Warning System) Analysis

Notebook-based EWS implementation with multiple stress scenarios

EWS Methodology
  • Indicators: GDP growth, PCE growth, Unemployment change
  • Standardization: StandardScaler normalization (mean=0, std=1)
  • Thresholds: Mean + 1.5 × Standard Deviation (K=1.5)
  • Warning Signals: GDP/PCE below threshold, Unemployment above threshold
  • EWS Score: Sum of warning signals (0-3 scale)
  • Scenarios: Mild, Moderate, Severe, and Custom stress scenarios

Macro Indicators Analysis

Advanced stress testing using standardized macro indicators with 1.5×Std Dev thresholds

Methodology
  • Data Preparation: Calculate percentage growth for GDP/consumption, differences for unemployment
  • Standardization: StandardScaler normalization (mean=0, std=1) for direct comparability
  • Thresholds: Mean + 1.5 × Standard Deviation for each indicator
  • Warning Signals: 1 for GDP/consumption below threshold, 1 for unemployment above threshold
  • Scenarios: Applied to historical data, forecasted values, and stress-test scenarios
